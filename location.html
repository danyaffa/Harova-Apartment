Location

Harova Apartment is located in a quiet street right next to the Jaffa Flea Market (Shuk HaPishpeshim) in Tel Aviv-Yafo.

From the apartment you can easily walk to:

  • Old City of Jaffa & Jaffa Port
  • Jaffa Clock Tower
  • Beachfront promenade towards Tel Aviv
  • Cafés, wine bars, restaurants and galleries
