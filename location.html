Location

Harova Apartment is in the Jaffa Flea Market area (Shuk HaPishpeshim), a historic and colourful neighbourhood in Tel Aviv-Yafo filled with vintage shops, designer boutiques, cafés and wine bars.

You’re a short walk from Old Jaffa, the port, Jaffa Clock Tower, and the beachfront promenade that leads all the way to central Tel Aviv.

Distances on foot

  • ≈ 3–5 minutes to Jaffa Flea Market
  • ≈ 8–10 minutes to Old Jaffa and Jaffa Port
  • ≈ 8–12 minutes to the beach and promenade
  • Short taxi or bus ride to central Tel Aviv
